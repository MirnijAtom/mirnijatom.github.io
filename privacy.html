Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how the DevelopFilm app handles user data.

Data Collection

DevelopFilm does not collect, store, or share any personal data. All data you enter, such as journal entries, is stored locally on your device and never transmitted to any server.

Third-Party Services

The app does not use analytics, advertisements, or any third-party tracking tools.

Internet Access

DevelopFilm does not require an internet connection for normal operation. All features work fully offline.

Data Security

Because your data remains entirely on your device, its security depends on your device’s security measures (e.g., passcode, Face ID, Touch ID).

Changes to This Policy

If the Privacy Policy changes in the future, this page will be updated accordingly.
